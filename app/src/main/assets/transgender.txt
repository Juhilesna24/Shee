The ministry of social justice and empowerment is giving finishing touches to an umbrella scheme which would be launched in the next financial year
The umbrella scheme would have five components , pre-matric and post-matric scholarship for transgender children, support for vocational training of transgenders, pension for out-of-work transgenders in the age group of 40 to 60 years and monthly monetary support for families with transgender children.
